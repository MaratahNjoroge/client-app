Our Agenda

Bring The Change You Want To See In Kenya

Corruption, tribalism, nepotism, greedy leaders, 7 trillion debt, and unemployment are some of the things that are wrong with Kenya

We have complained enough while we sit back and do nothing to make things better. A few ordinary men and women have decided to do what they can in their small capacities to make Kenya better. Is it in you to change the story of Kenya? Be part of bringing the change you want to see. Join us, introduce us to your networks, donate money, volunteer your time, knowledge, skills, ideas, and/or present yourself for elective office as the change candidate for the August 2022 general elections.
